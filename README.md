# SubWatch
A lightweight browser-based subscription tracker to manage renewals, free trials, reminders, and recurring expenses.

## 📺 SubWatch

Subscription services are everywhere—streaming platforms, cloud storage, productivity tools, software licenses, and many more. Keeping track of renewal dates, free trials, and recurring costs can quickly become overwhelming.

**SubWatch** is a lightweight browser-based subscription management tool that helps users keep all their subscriptions in one place. It allows users to track renewal dates, monitor recurring expenses, manage free trials, and receive reminders before subscriptions renew.

Built with simplicity and privacy in mind, SubWatch requires **no installation, no registration, and no subscription fees**. All data is stored locally in the browser, ensuring that your information remains private and under your control.

## ✨ Features

* ✅ Track unlimited subscriptions
* ✅ Monitor upcoming renewal dates
* ✅ Free trial tracking and expiration reminders
* ✅ Multi-currency support with exchange rates
* ✅ Categorize subscriptions by service type
* ✅ Subscription history and renewal timeline
* ✅ Cancellation links and notes
* ✅ Local storage support for offline usage
* ✅ Privacy-friendly — all data stays in your browser
* ✅ Clean and minimalist interface

Whether you're managing Netflix, Spotify, Adobe Creative Cloud, cloud services, or business software subscriptions, SubWatch helps you stay organized and avoid unexpected charges.
